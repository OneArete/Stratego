# Arete Chime — Sonic Identity Brief

Status: Direction Frozen  
Version: 0.1  
Owner: OneArete Brand, Product and Architecture Functions  

## Purpose

Arete Chime will become the original sonic signature of OneArete and Strategos.

It must be recognisable without becoming intrusive.

It is not background music and not a generic application sound.

## Core Structure

```text
Presence
↓
Ascent
↓
Resolution
```

Target duration:

```text
2.2 to 2.8 seconds
```

Nominal target:

```text
Approximately 2.5 seconds
```

## Emotional Character

The sound should communicate:

- presence;
- clarity;
- disciplined elevation;
- intelligence;
- calm;
- humanity;
- direction.

## Sparta and Ancient Greece

The reference should be subtle and culturally evocative rather than literal.

Potential timbral references:

- restrained bronze resonance;
- a very small lyre-like plucked element;
- an aulos-inspired breath colour used abstractly;
- minimal ritual percussion;
- open modal intervals associated with antiquity.

The final work must not imitate a known recording or copyrighted sonic logo.

## Prohibited Character

Arete Chime must not sound:

- militaristic;
- warlike;
- triumphalist;
- tourist-themed;
- like a sword or battle effect;
- like a Hollywood trailer;
- excessively epic;
- ominous;
- religiously prescriptive;
- similar to another company’s sonic identity.

Sparta should be suggested through discipline, restraint and bronze-like presence—not through aggression.

## Technical Requirements

The signature must remain recognisable:

- on an iPhone speaker;
- at low volume;
- without deep bass;
- without the visual opening;
- in mono;
- after audio compression;
- in a shorter derivative version.

Future deliverables:

```text
Primary Chime
Reduced Chime
Very Short Confirmation Motif
Lossless Master
Compressed Web Asset
Usage Rules
Loudness Specification
Accessibility and Silence Behaviour
```

## Product Use

Permitted initial uses:

- first opening of the day;
- first opening after installation;
- meaningful company release;
- beginning of Monthly Council where enabled.

It should not play:

- on every return to the application;
- during urgent or safety-critical interaction;
- when sound is disabled;
- when the experience would disturb the person;
- as a reward sound for compliance.

## Implementation Boundary

No temporary or stock sound should be inserted before the original composition is approved.

The current Strategos release contains only this specification.

## Foundational Test

> Does the sound evoke disciplined human elevation and a subtle memory of Ancient Greece while remaining calm, modern, original and unmistakably OneArete?

If the answer is no, it is not Arete Chime.
