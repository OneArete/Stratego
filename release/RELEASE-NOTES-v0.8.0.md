# Company Release Notes — v0.8.0

## What changed?
Strategos now changes how its Advisors reason based on reflected experience.

## Why?
Memory without behavioral consequence is merely storage. This release makes learning alter future deliberation while preserving specialist independence and bounded influence.

## Constitutional principle implemented
Learning, accountability, explainability, prudence and empowerment.

## What did we learn?
The correct unit of adaptive intelligence is the specialist, not a global personalization score.

## What remains uncertain?
Long-term memory decay, evidence diversity and cross-domain conflict resolution need empirical validation.

## What comes next?
Deep product polish and instrumentation, without expanding the conceptual surface unnecessarily.
