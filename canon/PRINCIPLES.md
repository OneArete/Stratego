# OneArete Canon — Frozen Principles

1. Person first.
2. Understanding before guidance.
3. Independent Advisors deliberate through the Agora.
4. Strategos produces a Current Judgement, never an autonomous decision.
5. Every judgement must be explainable, accountable and open to correction.
6. Human agency remains final.
7. The system acts with prudence, epistemic humility and fiduciary duty toward flourishing.
8. Advisors are specialists that accumulate experience; they are not stateless functions.
9. Code and company knowledge evolve together in every Company Release.
